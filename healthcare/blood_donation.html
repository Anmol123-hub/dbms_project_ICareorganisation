CRITERIA FOR
BLOOD DONATION

Overall health- The donor must be fit and healthy, and should not be suffering from transmittable diseases. Age and weight- The donor must be 18–65 years old and should weigh a minimum of 50 kg. Pulse rate- Between 50 and 100 without irregularities. Hemoglobin level- A minimum of 12-20.0 g/dL. Blood pressure- Diastolic: 50–100 mm Hg, Systolic: 100–180 mm Hg. Body temperature- Should be normal, with an oral temperature not exceeding 37 °C. The time period between successive blood donations should be more than 3 months.
