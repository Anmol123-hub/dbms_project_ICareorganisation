ABOUT ENVIRONMENT MANAGEMENT

The environment plays a significant role to support life on earth. But there are some issues that are causing damages to life and the ecosystem of the earth.

The first module deals with the waste management system which helps user in differentiating between various type waste and managing them and guiding the user to the nearby location where he/she can recycle them.

The second module deals with the pollution management system. This helps in calculating the level of pollution (Air Quality Index and Air Quality Forecast) in the atmosphere of user’s nearby location.

INTERESTING FACTS


PAPER WASTE

Paper comes from trees and can be recycled a maximum of six times, after which, its fibres become too weak to hold together. It means if the daily run of any country’s popular magazine or newspaper was to be recycled, it could save about 75,000 trees.

ALUMINIUM SCRAP

Unlike paper which has a limit as to the times, it can be recycled, aluminum can be recycled forever. Recycling a can made of aluminum produces energy enough to run a television for 3 hours. Aluminum is one of the most recycled materials on the market today.

PLASTIC GARBAGE

Plastic does not decompose easily either. They take up to 1000 years to decompose. When they are thrown into the water, they contribute to the deaths of thousands of marine animals, let alone plastic pollution and contaminating the water and soil.
